Your premier licensed adventure and hospitality partner in the Gateway to the Garhwal Himalayas. Experience thrill and tranquility perfectly combined.

Registered & Trusted Local Experts

Based right in the heart of Rishikesh, Uttarakhand, Krishna Travels (krishnatt.com) is a fully registered tours and travels operator. We specialize in crafting seamless, unforgettable journeys through the majestic foothills of India. Whether you seek the adrenaline surge of mountain outdoor activities or the deep calm of traditional spiritual stays, our local expertise ensures an authentic, safe, and beautifully organized adventure.

Our Premium Offerings

White Water Rafting

Conquer the world-famous rapids of the holy Ganges. From beginner-friendly stretches to challenging white water runs, our certified guides ensure professional safety equipment and a thrilling experience.

Tandem Paragliding

Soar like an eagle high above the lush green valleys of Rishikesh. Take flight with highly experienced tandem pilots and capture panoramic aerial views of the beautiful Himalayan foothills.

Yoga & Meditation Courses

Immerse yourself in authentic spiritual practices in the Yoga Capital of the World. We arrange authentic multi-day courses, meditation retreats, and intensive teacher training programs with respected local schools.

Ashram & Hotel Lodging

We tailor accommodations to match your specific path. Choose a serene, traditional ashram setting for spiritual introspection and yoga, or book a premium modern hotel room with stunning mountain views.

Station & Airport Transfers

Hassle-free pick-ups and drops across Uttarakhand. We provide reliable private vehicle connections directly from nearby railway stations (like Haridwar or Yog Nagari Rishikesh), local bus terminals, and Jolly Grant Airport.

Vehicle & Scooty Rentals

Explore the winding streets and hidden mountain viewpoints of Rishikesh completely at your own pace. Choose from our top-tier fleet of rental cars or popular local two-wheelers and automatic scooties.
